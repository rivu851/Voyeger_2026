# 🌍 Voyager — AI Driven Travel Assistant

Voyager is an **AI-powered smart travel platform** designed to revolutionize how people explore the world. It combines trip planning, real-time assistance, safety features, and immersive discovery into a **single seamless experience**.

---

## ✨ Features

### 🤖 AI Travel Assistant

* 24/7 AI chatbot for travel queries and planning
* Smart recommendations based on user preferences
* AI-generated destination insights and guides

### 🧭 Smart Travel Planning

* Personalized trip customization (adventure, business, medical, etc.)
* Seamless itinerary creation and booking assistance
* Budget optimization with AI-driven suggestions

### 🌐 Destination Discovery

* Explore both **popular places** and **hidden gems**
* Virtual tour guide for immersive previews
* Category-based exploration (hills, beaches, heritage, etc.)

### 🌦️ Weather Intelligence

* Real-time weather updates
* Comparison with user’s current location for better planning

### 🚨 Safety & Emergency Support

* Geo-fencing alerts for dangerous areas
* Nearby hospitals, police stations, and emergency contacts
* Live location sharing with trusted contacts

### 🛍️ Local Experience Integration

* Discover local markets & souvenirs
* Cultural events and festivals calendar
* Support for local businesses ("Vocal for Local")

### 📸 AI Image Analyzer

* Upload images or URLs
* Ask travel-related questions
* Get AI-powered contextual insights

* All-in-one platform (Planning + Booking + Safety + AI)
